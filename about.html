About Us

PVC pipe, short for Polyvinyl Chloride pipe, is a widely used plastic piping material known for its versatility and durability. It's made from a synthetic polymer and is commonly used in plumbing, drainage, and various industrial applications. PVC pipes are known for being lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and relatively easy to install and maintain. PVC is highly resistant to rust, corrosion, and chemical degradation, making it suitable for various environments.
