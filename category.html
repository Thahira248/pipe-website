Pipes

A long tube or hollow body for conducting a liquid, gas, or finely divided solid or for structural purposes Pipes can be made from different materials like metal, plastic, concrete, or fiberglass

Fittings

Components used to connect, join, or adapt sections of pipe, often for changing direction, regulating flow, or connecting to other components

Tanks

Tanks can model fluid storage, supply, and discharge within a system. They are crucial for managing water pressure, accommodating fluctuating demand, and providing emergency storage
